Frequently asked questions

Do I need to switch POS systems?

No. PulpIQ works with your existing Square, Toast, or Clover. Nothing to replace.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We encrypt all data, never share it with third parties, and you can request deletion at any time.

What if I can't export my data?

When you sign up, we email you a PDF guide with step-by-step instructions for Square, Toast, and Clover. Still stuck? Email us at hello@trypulp.co.

How does the free plan work?

Your first report is completely free — no credit card needed. You also get 2 free weekly uploads to try the tracking features. After that, PulpIQ Pro is $29/month for unlimited weekly analytics. Cancel anytime.

How is this different from Toast or Square?

Toast and Square show you raw sales numbers. PulpIQ analyzes your data to find waste, recommend order quantities, and track trends week over week. And your first report is free.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. If you can export a CSV from your POS (we send you a guide), you're good. Upload it and your report appears instantly.

What results can I expect?

You'll immediately see where you're over-ordering and how much it's costing you. Most cafes find hundreds of dollars in monthly savings just from adjusting their order quantities.

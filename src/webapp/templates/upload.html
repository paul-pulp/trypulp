Export a sales report from your cash register system

What we need in your CSV

  • Date and time of each sale
  • Item name and quantity
  • Price or total amount
  • Category (optional but helpful)

Most cash register systems include all of this. Just export your sales report as CSV.

How to export from your system

Square
  1. Log in to your Square Dashboard (squareup.com/dashboard)
  2. Go to Reports in the left sidebar
  3. Click Sales
  4. Set date range to the last 30-90 days
  5. Click Export (top right) → choose "Item Detail"
  6. Download the CSV file
Toast
  1. Log in to Toast Web (pos.toasttab.com)
  2. Go to Reports → Sales Summary
  3. Set date range to the last 30-90 days
  4. Click Export → choose "Transaction Detail"
  5. Download as CSV
Clover
  1. Log in to your Clover Web Dashboard
  2. Go to Reporting → Transactions
  3. Set date range to the last 30-90 days
  4. Click Export (top right)
  5. Download the CSV file

Using a different system? Look for "Sales Report" or "Transaction Report" → Export as CSV.

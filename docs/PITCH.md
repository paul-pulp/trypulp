*Use this when talking to cafe owners, sending cold outreach, or explaining the product in person.*

## The Opening (10 seconds)

**"Do you know exactly how much money your cafe wastes every month on food that doesn't sell?"**

Most cafe owners don't. They order based on gut feel, prepare for the busiest day, and throw away what doesn't sell. PulpIQ shows you the exact number — and tells you how to fix it.

---

## The Problem (30 seconds)

Every cafe has the same three blind spots:

1. **You're over-ordering.** You order croissants for your busiest Saturday, but most days you sell half that. The rest goes in the bin. Multiply that across your whole menu and it's hundreds of dollars a month.

2. **You don't know what's actually making you money.** Your POS has the data, but who has time to build spreadsheets? You're running a cafe, not an accounting firm.

3. **You can't see if things are getting better or worse.** Without tracking week over week, you're always guessing. Was last week good? Bad? You don't know until the bank statement hits.

---

## The Solution (30 seconds)

PulpIQ takes your sales data and gives you answers in seconds.

**You export your sales from Square, Toast, or Clover** (takes 2 minutes — we send you a step-by-step guide).

**You upload the file to trypulp.co** (drag and drop).

**You see your report instantly:**
- What's selling and what isn't
- Exactly how much you're wasting per month, item by item
- How many of each item you should actually be ordering
- Your milk usage broken down by type (dairy, oat, almond)
- Your busiest hours and best days
- How much you could save if you adjust your ordering

**Then you come back each week** with a new export and see if things are improving. Revenue up? Waste down? PulpIQ tracks it all.

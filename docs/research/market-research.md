## Independent Café Pain Points

### Financial Pressure
- **Net margins:** 10-15% (vs 15-25% for established chains)
- **Labor costs:** 25-35% of revenue
- **Coffee bean costs:** At record highs
- **60%+ close within 5 years**

### Operational Challenges

**1. Waste & Inventory**
- Milk spoilage (short shelf life, multiple types: whole, oat, almond, 2%)
- Pastry waste (daily bakery orders, end-of-day leftovers)
- Bean degradation (expensive, loses flavor over time)
- Seasonal demand swings (iced drinks summer, hot drinks winter)
- **Result:** 15-25% of inventory wasted

**2. Labor Management**
- Unpredictable traffic patterns
- Rush hour staffing (7-9am, 12-2pm)
- Overstaffing during slow periods
- Understaffing during unexpected rushes
- Manual scheduling based on "gut feel"
- **Result:** 25-35% labor inefficiency

**3. Customer Retention**
- No systematic VIP tracking
- Lost customers go unnoticed
- Generic loyalty programs (punch cards)
- No churn prediction
- **Result:** High customer acquisition costs

### Technology Adoption
- **28% have online ordering** (low penetration)
- **Friction:** Disconnected systems, complexity
- **Most use:** Square or Toast POS (basic features only)
- **Gap:** Not using advanced AI features

- Toast: **$69-165/month**

**Current spending on add-ons:**
- Labor scheduling (7shifts): **$30-135/month**
- Inventory (WISK): **$150-250/month**
- **Total stack:** $225-550/month

**PulpIQ pricing ($199/month):**
- **30-65% cheaper** than competitive stack
- **3-4x cost** of Square alone (affordable step-up)
- **Same ballpark** as Clover or Toast (familiar price point)

### Alternative Pricing Models (to test)

**Option 1: Flat Rate**
- $199/month per location
- Pros: Predictable, easy to understand
- Cons: Doesn't scale with value

**Option 2: Success-Based**
- 2-3% of monthly savings
- Pros: Aligns incentives, easy "yes"
- Cons: Harder to calculate, inconsistent revenue

**Option 3: Tiered**
- Basic: $149/month (reports only)
- Pro: $199/month (reports + consultation)
- Enterprise: $299/month (custom analysis)
- Pros: Upsell opportunities
- Cons: Complexity for small market

**Recommendation:** Start with Option 1 (flat $199/month), test Option 2 during pilot

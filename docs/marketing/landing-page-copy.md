## FAQ

### Q: Do I need to switch POS systems?
**A:** Nope! PulpIQ works with your existing Square, Toast, or Clover. No rip-and-replace.

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes. We encrypt all data, never share it with third parties, and you can delete it anytime.

### Q: What if I can't export my data?
**A:** We'll help! We have step-by-step guides for Square, Toast, and Clover. Still stuck? Book a 10-minute call and we'll walk you through it.

### Q: What happens after 6 weeks?
**A:** You decide. If PulpIQ saved you money, continue for $199/month. If not, cancel—no hard feelings, no hidden fees.

### Q: How is this different from Toast or Square?
**A:** Toast is $600+/month and requires switching your entire POS. Square doesn't have AI forecasting. PulpIQ is coffee-shop-specific, works with what you already have, and costs 60% less.

### Q: Do I need to be technical?
**A:** Not at all. If you can export a CSV file (we'll show you how), you're good to go. Everything else is handled via email—no dashboards, no complex software.

### Q: Can I try it for just one location first?
**A:** Absolutely! Many cafés start with one location and expand once they see results.

### Q: What kind of results can I expect?
**A:** Our pilot cafés saved $800-1,200/month on average through waste reduction (20-30%) and labor optimization (10-15%). Your results will vary based on your current efficiency.

- **Secondary:** Cream (#F5F5DC)
- **Accent:** Green (#2D5016 - growth, savings)
- **Text:** Dark gray (#333333)
- **Background:** Off-white (#FAFAFA)

### Typography:
- **Headlines:** DM Sans (Bold, 48-72px)
- **Body:** Inter or System Font (Regular, 16-18px)
- **CTA Buttons:** DM Sans (Bold, 18px)

### CTA Button Style:
- **Background:** Green (#2D5016)
- **Text:** White
- **Border radius:** 8px
- **Padding:** 16px 32px
- **Hover:** Darker green (#1F3510)

### Imagery:
- Use authentic coffee shop photos (not stock)
- Show diverse café owners
